Personal Profile

I obtained a Computer Science degree in 2013 and i am PhD-student from 2015 at University of Vigo ,a being member of the SING research group (Next Generation Computer Systems Group) since 2013. Currently I am working in the field of development of Intelligent Tools for Integrated and Specialised Views of Clinical Bibliome at University of Vigo, under Dr. Anália Lourenço García and Dr. Martin Krallinger supervision.

The combination of Text mining techniques, data mining techniques and Life sciences (especially Bioinformatics) defines my main research line. I am also specialized in the development of web applications (PHP, SQL,Javascript, css, etc) and java applications.
